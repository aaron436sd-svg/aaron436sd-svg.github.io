Rólunk

Akácfa Étkezde has been a neighborhood fixture since 1998. Founded by the Kovács family, we serve honest, home-style Hungarian food at fair prices.

Our Story

What started as a small family lunch counter quickly became a favored stop for local workers and families. We cook from traditional recipes handed down within the family and from neighbors who shared their secrets. Our mission is to keep Hungarian home cooking alive — simple, tasty and affordable.

The Team

Head cook: Mrs. Erzsébet Kovács — a decades-long cook of soulful, comforting dishes. Manager: Gábor Kovács — keeps the kitchen running and greets guests every day. A small, friendly staff ensures every meal is served with care.

Why People Love Us

  • Házias ízek — home-cooked flavors and comforting portions.
  • Local atmosphere — welcoming staff and familiar faces.
  • Affordable daily menus — perfect for a weekday lunch.
